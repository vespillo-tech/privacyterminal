March 2026 marks the activation of the EU AI Act's ban on real-time biometric identification systems in publicly accessible spaces. The regulation prohibits emotion recognition in workplaces and educational institutions, and bans social scoring systems, with narrow exceptions for law enforcement in cases of serious crime.
