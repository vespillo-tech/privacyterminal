Kentucky HB 692 passed the House with a unanimous 92-0 vote on March 17, 2026. The bill amends the Kentucky Consumer Data Protection Act to classify ACR data — the technology smart TVs use to identify what you're watching — as "sensitive data" requiring explicit consent before collection. If signed by the governor, it takes effect July 1, 2027.
